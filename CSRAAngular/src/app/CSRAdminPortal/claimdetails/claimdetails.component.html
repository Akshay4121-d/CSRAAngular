What is your Basic Information?What is your Claim Initiation Information?

This is a combination of key data points taken from parts A, B and C of the initial claim form. It includes your name, social security number, mailing address, work history, medical data, and your employer’s statement of work history and wage earnings. It is any information you provided on the PFL-1 claim form, the leave-type specific form (PFL 2, 3 or 4), and/or any supporting documentation/certifications, required to initiate your claim, e.g. your name, social security number, mailing address, leave type, your employer’s statement of work history, wage earnings, or documents such as your child’s birth certificate for bonding leave.

What is Medical Certification?

Benefit payments are directly related to your working status and Medical Certification. We ask for updates during your disability claim to ensure that benefits are being paid for the appropriate periods.
We may ask you to submit updated medical information to us during your disability claim. Prognoses often change during care and treatment of disabling conditions, and that can impact when your health care provider feels you should be medically able to return to work. We’ll always alert you when new medical is required, and will send the Medical Certification Form to you by your preferred delivery method.
